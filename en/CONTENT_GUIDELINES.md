# Content & Community Guidelines

**Last updated:** 26 December 2025

Favz is a platform for collecting, organizing, and sharing inspirations, ideas, and useful content.  
To keep Favz safe and welcoming, all users must follow these Content & Community Guidelines.

These rules apply to **all user-generated content**, including text, links, images, lists, and items â€” especially content made public.

---

## 1. General Principles
By using Favz, you agree to:
- Share content responsibly
- Respect other users
- Comply with applicable laws
- Follow these Guidelines at all times

If you are unsure whether content is allowed, **do not post it publicly**.

---

## 2. Prohibited Content
You may **not** upload, share, or make public any content that includes or promotes:

### Sexual Content
- Pornography or explicit sexual content
- Fetish content intended for sexual arousal
- **Any sexual content involving minors** (zero tolerance)

### Violence & Harm
- Graphic violence or physical injury
- Threats of violence
- Promotion of self-harm or suicide
- Terrorist or extremist content

### Hate & Harassment
- Hate speech targeting individuals or groups
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- Discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability

### Illegal or Harmful Activity
- Illegal goods or services
- Drug trafficking or instructions
- Fraud, scams, or deceptive practices
- Malware, phishing, or malicious links

### Intellectual Property Violations
- Copyrighted material you do not own or have rights to
- Pirated software, media, or download links

### Spam & Abuse
- Repetitive or misleading content
- Promotional spam
- Automated or bulk posting

---

## 3. Public vs Private Content
- **Private content** is visible only to you and carries lower risk
- **Public content** is visible to others and may be reported

If you choose to make content public, you accept increased responsibility for what you share.

---

## 4. Images
Images uploaded to Favz must:
- Be appropriate for a general audience
- Not contain nudity, sexual acts, or extreme violence
- Not depict abuse, exploitation, or illegal activities

Images may be automatically or manually reviewed.

---

## 5. Links
You are responsible for any links you share.

Links must not:
- Lead to pornographic or violent websites
- Promote illegal activities
- Mislead users or hide malicious intent

Reported links may be removed or blocked.

---

## 6. Reporting Content
Favz provides tools to report public content that violates these Guidelines.

When content is reported, we may:
- Temporarily hide the content
- Review it manually
- Remove it permanently
- Take action against the responsible account

Abuse of the reporting system may also result in action.

---

## 7. Enforcement
We reserve the right to:
- Remove or restrict content at any time
- Suspend or permanently ban accounts
- Take action without prior notice

Repeated or severe violations may result in permanent removal from Favz.

Moderation decisions are final.

---

## 8. Responsibility
Favz does not pre-moderate all content.  
Users are solely responsible for the content they upload or share.

However, we take reports seriously and act promptly when violations are identified.

---

## 9. Updates to These Guidelines
We may update these Guidelines from time to time.

Continued use of Favz after changes means you accept the updated version.

---

## 10. Contact
If you have questions or concerns regarding these Guidelines, please contact us through the App or via our official support channels.

---
