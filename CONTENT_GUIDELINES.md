# Content Guidelines

**Last Updated: December 26, 2025**

## Purpose

These Content Guidelines outline the standards for content shared on our platform. By using our services, you agree to follow these guidelines.

## General Principles

We strive to maintain a safe, respectful, and inclusive community. All content should:
- Be respectful and civil
- Comply with applicable laws
- Respect others' rights and dignity

## Prohibited Content

The following types of content are not allowed:

### Illegal Content
- Content that violates local, national, or international laws
- Content that promotes illegal activities
- Pirated or counterfeit materials

### Harmful Content
- Violence, gore, or graphic content
- Content that promotes self-harm or suicide
- Exploitation or abuse of minors
- Non-consensual intimate imagery

### Harassment and Hate
- Targeted harassment or bullying
- Hate speech based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Threats of violence

### Misleading Content
- Impersonation of others
- Misinformation that could cause harm
- Spam or deceptive practices
- Phishing or scams

### Adult Content
- Sexually explicit content (may be restricted rather than prohibited depending on community)
- Content involving minors in any sexual context

### Intellectual Property Violations
- Copyright infringement
- Trademark violations
- Unauthorized use of others' work

## Content Standards

### Be Authentic
- Represent yourself honestly
- Don't misrepresent your identity or affiliations
- Disclose sponsored or paid content

### Be Respectful
- Treat others with respect
- Engage in constructive dialogue
- Respect others' privacy

### Be Responsible
- Think before you post
- Consider the impact of your content
- Report violations you encounter

## Enforcement

### Reporting
Users can report content that violates these guidelines. We review all reports and take appropriate action.

### Actions We May Take
- Content removal
- Warning notices
- Account suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Reporting to law enforcement when necessary

### Appeals
If you believe content was removed in error, you may appeal the decision through our support channels.

## Age-Appropriate Content

- Content should be appropriate for the intended audience
- Age-restricted content must be properly labeled
- Minors' safety is a top priority

## Spam and Manipulation

- Don't artificially inflate engagement
- Don't engage in coordinated inauthentic behavior
- Don't manipulate our systems

## Privacy and Personal Information

- Don't share others' personal information without consent
- Respect privacy expectations
- Be careful with sensitive information

## Community Standards

We encourage:
- Positive and constructive interactions
- Diverse perspectives and healthy debate
- Creative and original content
- Helping and supporting other users

## Content Moderation

- Automated systems and human reviewers monitor content
- Context matters in moderation decisions
- We continuously improve our processes

## Updates to Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated periodically. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of updated guidelines.
